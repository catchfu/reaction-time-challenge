Reaction Time Challenge
Test your reflexes with a visually engaging reaction game

Concept:
A simple yet addictive game that tests players' reaction times. The screen changes color or displays a shape, and players must click as quickly as possible. The game tracks reaction times and provides analytics on performance.

Implementation:
Simple React interface with animation effects
Precise timing mechanisms for accurate measurements
Performance tracking and statistics
Global leaderboard integration
Monetization:
Ad integration between game sessions
Premium challenge modes
Detailed analytics and improvement tips
Why It Works:
Extremely simple to implement
Highly addictive gameplay
Competitive element drives engagement
Appeals to casual gamers
