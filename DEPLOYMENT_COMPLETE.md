### **How It Works**:

3. **Deploy** → Automatically deploys to GitHub Pages
4. **Live Site** → Available at https://catchfu.github.io/reaction-time-challenge/

### **Workflow Location**:
- File: `.github/workflows/deploy.yml`
- Triggers: Push to master branch
- Runtime: ~2-3 minutes
